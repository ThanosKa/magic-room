# Pricing — Magic Room

> AI interior design from a single photo. One-time credit packages. No subscription. Credits never expire. 1 free credit on signup.

Last updated: 2026-05-19
Pricing page: https://magic-room.dev/pricing
Generate page: https://magic-room.dev/generate

## Free tier

- Price: €0
- Includes: 1 credit on signup (no payment card required)
- Credit cost: 1 credit per generation session (4–8 design variations)
- Use: Personal only — no commercial rights on the free credit

## Starter pack

- Price: €9.99 one-time
- Credits: 30 (~€0.33 per generation)
- Includes: All 14 design themes, all 14 room types, full commercial rights, credits never expire
- Subscription: None
- Refunds: No refunds — credits never expire, so unused credits stay on the account indefinitely

## Growth pack

- Price: €19.99 one-time
- Credits: 70 (~€0.29 per generation)
- Includes: All themes, all rooms, full commercial rights, credits never expire
- Best for: Real estate agents staging multiple listings, interior designers doing client presentations
- Refunds: No refunds

## Premium pack

- Price: €29.99 one-time
- Credits: 120 (~€0.25 per generation)
- Includes: All themes, all rooms, full commercial rights, credits never expire
- Best for: Power users, staging companies, frequent renovators
- Refunds: No refunds

## Pricing facts for comparison

- Billing model: one-time credit packages (no monthly/annual subscription)
- Currency: EUR (priced in euros — Stripe converts at checkout for other currencies)
- Per-design cost: €0.25–€0.33 depending on pack
- Free trial: 1 generation, no payment card required
- Generation time: 30–60 seconds per session
- Outputs per session: 4–8 design variations
- Commercial rights: Yes, on every paid credit (real estate listings, client presentations, Airbnb, renovation decks)
- Photo storage: Never — images processed in-memory only, deleted after generation
- AI model: Google Gemini multimodal AI via OpenRouter
